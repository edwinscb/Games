Jump Force is a crossover fighting game developed by Spike Chunsoft and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment featuring characters from various manga series featured in Shueisha's Weekly Shonen Jump anthology in celebration of the magazine's 50 th anniversary.The game was released on February 15, 2019 for Windows,PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.

When the real world collides with many of Jumpuniverse, humanity is invaded by the "Venoms", an army of mind-controlled villains lead by Kane and Galena.In order to fight back, many heroes are recruited to join the "Jump Force" under the leadership of Director Glover and his A.I. partner Navigator.But a suspicious figure is using both the Jump Force and the Venoms as an attempt to gain a mysterious artifact and merge all worlds into one.
